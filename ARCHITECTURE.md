# Drift

Version: 1.0

---

# Vision

Drift transforms life's waiting moments into emotionally meaningful AI experiences.

Instead of helping people consume more content, Drift helps people experience something memorable during the forgotten moments between destinations.

---

# Product Category

AI Experience Engine

---

# Problem

People spend hours waiting.

Airports.
Hospitals.
Queues.
Coffee shops.
Interviews.
Train stations.

Most people automatically open social media because nothing better exists.

Those moments disappear without creating value.

---

# Solution

Drift understands:

- where the user is
- how much time they have
- how they are likely feeling

It then designs a personalized AI experience that fits that exact moment.

The objective is emotional transformation rather than information delivery.

---

# Product Principles

1. Experience before information.

2. AI reasons before generating.

3. Every experience should fit the available time.

4. The interface should disappear behind the experience.

5. Every session should leave the user feeling different.

---

# Target Users

People who have unexpected free time and want something more meaningful than endless scrolling.
